Broccoli pakora

Ingredients

  • Gram flour
  • Rice flour
  • Chilly powder
  • Salt
  • Asafoetida
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Add 2 tbl spoons of gram flour
  2. Add 2 tbl spoons of rice flour
  3. Add half tbl sppon of chilly powder and salt
  4. Add a pinch of asafoetida
  5. Add water for a smooth consistency
